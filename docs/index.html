Features

⚔

VibeScore 0–100

Composite score from commits, AI usage, streaks, and tool diversity. Capped inputs prevent gaming.

🏆

Global Leaderboard

See your daily and weekly rank. "You climbed 3 ranks" / "Defeating 97% of devs today."

🔍

Zero Config Detection

Reads local files automatically. No API keys needed for AI tools. Just install and go.

🔒

Privacy First

Only a single number (your score) leaves your machine. No code, no prompts, no usernames.

How Scoring Works

Git Commits 35 pts caps at 20 commits
AI Tokens (all tools) 35 pts caps at 500K tokens
Streak 15 pts caps at 14 days
Tool Diversity 15 pts caps at 3 tools

Privacy

What leaves your machine:

  • A single VibeScore number (0–100)
  • A hashed anonymous ID (not reversible)

What never leaves your machine:

  • Your code, commits, or repo names
  • AI prompts, messages, or conversations
  • API keys, tokens, or credentials
  • Your username, email, or identity
